pakij is a procedurally-generated physics game where you play as a QC-rejected box. Navigate an increasingly dangerous factory and uncover hidden areas and secrets as you try to escape.

pakij was originally conceived for Game Jam IX at Stevens Institute of Technology, where it won First Place. This current version of the game is a modernization and content update aimed at expanding it beyond a five-minute experience, and will experience regular updates going forward.

Made with Unity, and includes contributions by Ben Blease and Alex Massenzio.
