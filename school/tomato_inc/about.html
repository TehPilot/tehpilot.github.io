About Us

Tomato Inc. - A love story

Tomato Inc. consists of 5 college students and many interns working with a small budget to create the best possible smartphone. We started out with $2000 for hardware and have $300 a year for maintenance of our website. Using the resources available to us, we were able to make all that you see before you! A company, a web page, and the world’s greatest phone.
